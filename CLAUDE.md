## About the Camp
- Family camp on an island in Georgian Bay, Pointe au Baril, Ontario
- ~53 member families, all descended from Annis Richardson who bought the island in 1926
- **Centennial year: 1926–2026**
- Audience: existing member families (not public-facing marketing)
